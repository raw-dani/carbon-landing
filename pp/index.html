DEFINITIONS

“Personal Data” is any information that relates to an identified or identifiable living individual.

“User” or “Data Subject” is the identified or identifiable living individual to whom personal data relates. In this Agreement, the Data Subject is also referred to as the User.

“Usage Data” is any data about how a product or service is used.

“Data Controller” determines the purposes for which and the means by which your personal data is processed.

“Data Processors” process personal data only on behalf of the controller. This refers to a third party external to CarbonX.

“Cookies” are text files with small pieces of data that are used to identify your computer as you use a network.

“Service” or “Site” means the website operated by CarbonX, https://carbonxco.com/ and its derivative sites.
